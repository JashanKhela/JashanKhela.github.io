OOber   A group project for our senior software design class inspired by our collective frustration with the current state of our University's ride share system. We want to create a tool for people to be able to ride share with other students but also a tool which allows a student to be the host of the ride. This will be a simple planning platform where users can browse a list of available rides and add rides for others to join. We expect further communication to take place outside the app and are interested in the simplicity of its humble feature set, and its potential to encourage offline communication and activity.
